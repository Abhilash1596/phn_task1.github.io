Reservation Conditions

Table are available for two hours of such shorter time communicated to you in ypur booking confirmation, after which they will be rebooked for another customer. Please contact us if you would like to stay at your table for longer than two hours and, if we have space available, we will do our best to accomodate you. For tables booked at 12:00 pm or later, or 07:30pm or later, your table will not be re-booked, but please do let us know if you'd like to stay for longer than two hours.

We respectfully ask that you are ready to be seated at the time of your reservation to ensure the best possible level of service to all of our guests. If you are running late, we kindly ask that you notify us by telephone at your earliest convenience. Your table will be held for 15 minutes after which time it may be allocated to another customer.

Cancellation Policy

We require at least four hours notice for every cancellation. For bookings cancelled with less than four hours notice, we will charge Rs100 per person if we are unable to resell the table. In the event that you do not show up, we may charge Rs150 per person. We also request a deposits to guarentee a table ona a busy days. We are sorry that cancellation charges and deposits have become necessary, but we have had too many "no-shows" resulting in empty tables we could have reserved for other customers.

Table Allocation

Due to the large number of requests, unfortunately we cannot guarantee windows tables or specific aresa when taking reservations, but we will do our best to accomodate your requests. Your understanding is appreciated.

As a guide, if you choose to dine early and leave your table by 07:00pm or later, or book a table from 07:30, there is a higher chance we are able to offer you a window table/ a specific area of the restaruant. However, we cannot always guarantee this availablity due to other bookings and table configurations.
